The Best Movies by Werner

My top 3 movies of all-time.

  1. Dragon Ball Z

    Not a movie but it is my favourite anime. Not only having the best action scenes in anime, but one of the best 90s style art work.

  2. Fight Club

    An absolutely entertaining movie with an unexpected twist. Overall one of the best movies I have watched

  3. Interstellar

    This is the best Sci-Fi movie that I have watched so far I love it. The cinematography was sensational.
